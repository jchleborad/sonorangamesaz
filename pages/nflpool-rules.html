🏈 Official Rules — NFL 2025 Season Pool

Welcome to the NFL 2025 Pool! The goal is simple: pick the most winners each week — and throughout the season — to earn your way into the prize pool. Here’s how it works:

📌 How to Play

  • Each week, you'll receive a form listing all scheduled games.
  • Pick the team you think will win each matchup.
  • One Monday Night game will include a tie-breaker: predict the combined final score for both teams.
  • Your picks must be submitted before the posted deadline, typically prior to the first game kickoff each week.

🧠 Making Picks

  • Submissions are made via Google Form each week (link provided by the commissioner).
  • Picks can be changed until the form closes.
  • If no form is submitted, you receive zero points for the week.

📅 Deadlines & Timing

  • Deadlines are typically Thursday before the first game, or Sunday morning when no Thursday game is played.
  • Forms are reset weekly and re-open on Tuesday at noon PST for the next week’s picks.

🧮 Scoring System

There are two components to your season score:

🧱 Metric 💯 Points 📈 Max Value
Correct Picks 1 point per correct game pick 272 total games
Weekly Wins (Most Correct) 10 points per weekly win 18 total weeks
Season Score = Picks + (Weekly Wins × 10) Max Score: 452 points

🎯 Tiebreakers

  1. Closest to tie-breaker (total score guess) for that week.
  2. Most weekly wins.
  3. Highest single-week score.
  4. Coin flip (commissioner discretion if needed).

🏆 Payouts

  • The Top 5 players at the end of the season will receive payouts.
  • Payout percentages will be shared once all entries are confirmed.
  • In the event of a tie for 5th, tiebreakers above will determine the payout position.

❗ Important Notes

  • Make sure you use the same player name each week.
  • Missed weeks cannot be made up.
  • All decisions by the commissioner are final.
